Find Foreign Food is a brand spanking new web application that lets you search for almost ANY kind of cuisine in almost ANY area! It does this with the help of Foursquare.

Read More

Why can't you just use Foursquare you ask?


Well, you totally could and that would be fine, but Find Foreign Food features a few pretty neat advantages, stroll over to the About page where you can read all about them in more detail, but are the main ones:

Offline First

This is the showstopper of this project. Achieving a fully offline cacheable and accessible web application has been my goal throughout the span of my Creative Technologies Project time. All the research I have carried out has contributed to this feature, and I really hope you love it!

Fully responsive

As with the rest of the web, websites and web apps should be accessible from any kind of device - okay, maybe not a Nokia 3310 but you get my point. Enjoy Foreign foods on mobile, tablet and desktop.

Find almost anything!

As I mentioned, Find Foreign Food uses Foursquare to carry out its searches, and Foursquare is friggin' massive! So don't be shy on what you search.
